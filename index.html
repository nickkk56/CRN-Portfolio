I’m Ceren Öztürk, a  graduate of Bahçeşehir University, Class of 2025, with a deep passion for designing spaces that invite stories to unfold. I see architecture not just as construction, but as a narrative— shaped by people, place, and purpose. My design approach is rooted in curiosity and imagination, blending function with feeling.

I am particularly drawn to landscape architecture and sustainability, always seeking ways to create harmony between the built environment and nature. Music, art, and the natural world are constant sources of inspiration in my process, helping me shape spaces that feel alive, thoughtful, and human. With a keen eye for atmosphere and a heart full of ideas, I design with both intention and emotion.
